Brands need to ask themselves: am I throwing a chatbot together just for the sake of technology? Or can I maximize the potential of chatbots as part of a greater messaging strategy? Somewhere between the frothy overhype and premature pessimism about chatbots is a sound, strategic place for them in the modern customer experience.
